ACCORDING TO ACCEPTED DEFINITIONS, A CITY IS SIMPLY A LARGE TOWN, BUT WHEN THE WORD "CITY" IS USED TO DESCRIBE AN AREA WHERE HUMANS LIVE CERTAIN IMPLICATIONS ARISE.

CITIES INCREASE THE COMFORTABLE POPULATION PER SQUARE METER IN A CERTAIN AREA OF LAND, ALLOWING MORE HUMANS TO EXIST IN THE SAME COLUMN OF AREA. HIGHRISES, SKYSCRAPERS AND APARMENTS INCREASE THE POPULATION PER SQUARE METER. IF THE WORLD EVENTUALLY HAD TO EXIST IN AN AREA AS SMALL AS SOUTH KOREA, LIKE THE KOWLOON WALLED CITY, WOULD IT BE A COMFORTABLE PLACE TO LIVE?

A LIFE IN A CITY SUCH AS THIS WOULD BE DIFFICULT TO ENFORCE LAWS IN. FINDING A PLACE TO CALL HOME CALMLY WOULD BE DIFFICULT. BUT IN THIS CITY, DREAMS AND ASPIRATIONS WOULD PERSIST, AND SELF-DETERMINATION WOULD REIGN SUPREME.
